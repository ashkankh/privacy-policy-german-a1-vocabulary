Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

German A1 Vocabulary ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the App and how that information is handled.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores learning-related information locally on your device, including:

  • Learning progress
  • Completed lessons and quizzes
  • Bookmarked words
  • App preferences and settings
  • Backup and restore data created by the user

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, including device identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostic information, and advertising-related usage data.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Data Security

The App does not maintain user accounts or store personal information on developer-controlled servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not specifically directed to children under 13 years of age.

Your Choices

  • Reset learning progress within the App
  • Delete locally stored data by uninstalling the App
  • Create and restore local backup files

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
